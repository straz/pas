The BizCase Demo

The demo version of BizCase is limited in only two respects.

  • You cannot save any files you create, and
  • Most of the help system is not included.

System Requirements

  • Microsoft Windows 3.0 or later
  • 4 MB of memory (8 MB recommended)
  • At least 10 MB of free space on your hard drive
  • For optional formatted printing: Microsoft Word 2.0 or 6.0

Download and Installation

  1. Download BizCaseDemo.zip (3.9 MB) to your computer by clicking on this button.

  2. Use WinZip, NetZip, or other Zip utility to extract the installation.
  3. Double-click on SETUP.EXE in the extracted directory.
  4. Then follow the prompts to complete installation.

    If you have questions, write info@strassmann.com.

    Running the Demo

    After installation, click the BizCase icon. Read the two introductory screens. Then click one of the three buttons on the third screen:

    • Start. This opens a complete example business case for a medical supply organization. Use this example to learn about each of the BizCase modules.

    • Help. This shows the table of contents for the complete BizCase help system.

    • Movie. The movie starts with a conceptual overview of BizCase and then walks you through the key features of the software.
